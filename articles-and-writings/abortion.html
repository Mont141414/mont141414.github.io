My Thoughts on Abortion

For this argument to be applied correctly, we will assume that...

1 - One wants a healthy society as well as their members
2 - The society in question has no problems regarding sexual education
3 - The basics of logic and their axioms are valid and true
4 - At least one 100% efficient contraceptive method exists (e.g. vasectomy, abstinence)

Given these assumptions, the argument is as follows:

Affirmation 1 - Pregnancy is avoidable through effective methods when undesired[P]
Affirmation 2 - There exist women who become pregnant unintentionally and unforcefully (WPUUs)[P]
Affirmation 3 - People who get into undesired yet avoidable situations are irresponsible and irrational[P]
Affirmation 4 - WPUUs are irresponsible and irrational[1, 2, 3]
Affirmation 5 - For a healthy society, mothers should be responsible and rational[P]
Affirmation 6 - For a healthy society, WPUUs shouldn't be mothers[4, 5]
Affirmation 7 - If someone should not be a mother, then their children need to be taken away and future pregnancies prevented[P]
Affirmation 8 - For a healthy society, WPUUs should have their children taken away and future pregnancies prevented[6, 7]
